PASE

The Purdue Alumni Student Experience (PASE) is exactly what its name claims. As the student membership of the Purdue Alumni Association, Purdue students receive the same benefits as our alumni members with the addition of an extraordinary experience. Learn more here.

Senior Year Experience

We want to celebrate your senior year and provide resources to help you transition into life after college. The Senior Year Experience is for all seniors at Purdue featuring events and resources for you throughout the year. Click here for more details.
